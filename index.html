Project Proposal

Basket

Basket is a multi-platform couponing program designed to provide grocery shopping assistance to people struggling with food insecurity or using EBT to purchase their groceries. The design will focus on readability and usability to ensure accessibility. This project will serve as an opportunity to hone my web design skills, strengthen my graphic design, and encourage the creation of real-world programs designed to help the community.

Goals

  • Define a recognizable brand
  • Effective navigation with easy backtracking and user-agency centered
  • Create a quality Figma prototype
  • Showcase aspects of the concept that would most help struggling people
  • Design a digital environment that is both empathetic and discreet for users who may be uncomfortable displaying their situation

Technical Requirements

  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop platforms
  • Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA standards)
  • Integration with existing grocery store coupon databases
  • User authentication and data privacy protection
  • Barcodes for easy scanning and identification

Audience Information

Skills to Improve

  • Improve Your UX Design Skills

Timeline & Design Sprints

  • Week 1: Wireframing and Prototyping
  • Week 2-3: Visual Design and Branding
  • Week 4: Feedback and Finalization
